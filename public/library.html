Understanding COAs
🔬
What is a 6-Panel COA?
Look for vendors running all 6 tests on every batch
💧
HPLC Purity
High-Performance Liquid Chromatography. The gold standard for measuring peptide purity. Trident requires 99%+. Most suppliers only report this one.
🌊
UV-Vis Spectroscopy
Confirms the compound absorbs light at the correct wavelength — verifying you have the right molecule, not just something pure.
⚖️
Beer-Lambert Assay
Calculates the actual concentration in the vial. Confirms the stated dose is what's actually in there.
⚠️
ICP-MS Heavy Metals
Tests for lead, mercury, arsenic, cadmium. Critical for compounds sourced overseas. Most suppliers skip this entirely.
🦠
TAMC (Microbial)
Total Aerobic Microbial Count. Confirms the product is sterile and safe to inject. Non-negotiable for SubQ use.
🍄
TYMC (Yeast & Mold)
Tests for yeast and fungal contamination specifically. Paired with TAMC for full microbial safety clearance.
