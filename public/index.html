Questions

Answers to the things beginners always ask.

Do I need any experience with peptides?
+
Not at all. PepTrak was built with complete beginners in mind. The intake questionnaire asks about your experience level and injection comfort — the protocol it generates is calibrated to where you are right now.
Are peptides safe? What about side effects?
+
Peptides are research compounds. Most commonly used peptides like BPC-157, TB-500, and GH secretagogues have strong safety records at proper doses. PepTrak uses conservative dosing for beginners, includes a side effect logging system, and Alex is trained to triage side effects. PepTrak is for research use only and is not medical advice.
What if I'm scared of injections?
+
The intake asks specifically about injection comfort. If you're nervous, your protocol will prioritize intranasal compounds that require no injections at all. For injectable protocols, Alex walks you through first-injection guidance step by step.
How is this different from forum research?
+
Forum protocols are written for anonymous users with no knowledge of your body weight, health history, bloodwork, or experience level. PepTrak uses your actual data to build something specific to you — and you have an advisor available 24/7 who knows your full context.
Where do I get the actual peptides?
+
Your protocol includes a direct link to order from Trident Labs, a verified peptide supplier with 6-panel COA testing on every batch. PepTrak doesn't sell compounds — we connect you to verified sources.
